Mileva

Elsa

Although this Elsa did not come from Frozen, her loving care was powerful enough to nurse Albert Einstein back to his health during 1917 to 1920. Elsa already had two daughters, Ilse and Margot, from her first marriage. The couple then moved to Haberlandstrasse 5 in Berlin.

Institute for Advanced Study, Princeton, New Jersey
Albert taught physics at the Institute for Advanced Study at Princeton, New Jersey until his death on 18 April 1955. He was still writing about quantum physics hours before he died. He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics.
